help_outline How to Play

Objective: Be the first player to reach the opposite side of the board.

  • On your turn, you can either move your pawn or place a wall.
  • Pawns move one square at a time (horizontally or vertically).
  • If an opponent's pawn is adjacent, you can jump over it.
  • Walls block movement and must not completely block off a player's path to their goal.
